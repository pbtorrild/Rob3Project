\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Preface}
A study group consisting of third-semester students from Aalborg University created this project. The project report is the result of immense research, implementation and testing done in the fall of 2018 from primo September to the $18^{th}$ of December.  The report will present a solution to the problems of creating an electrical prosthesis from a robotics students perspective. The project group thanks MSc. John Hansen, from the Department of Health Science and Technology at Aalborg University, for providing great insights and supervision to the group during the cause of this project and recognise that without him this project would not have bin the same.
